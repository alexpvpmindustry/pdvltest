PDVL Medical Check-up

If you're applying for a Private Hire Car Driver's Vocational Licence (PDVL) in Singapore or renewing your existing license, you'll need to undergo a medical check-up to ensure you meet the medical requirements for driving. Here's what you need to know about the PDVL medical check-up:

Medical Requirements

PDVL applicants and renewal candidates are required to meet the following medical requirements:

  • Good visual acuity
  • Normal colour vision
  • No significant hearing loss
  • No history of epilepsy or other conditions that may cause sudden loss of consciousness
  • No uncontrolled diabetes or hypertension
  • No significant psychiatric conditions

What to Expect

The PDVL medical check-up typically involves a physical examination and a series of tests to assess your vision, hearing, and overall health. The examination may include:

  • Eye exam
  • Hearing test
  • Blood pressure check
  • Blood and urine tests
  • Medical history review

How to Prepare

To prepare for your PDVL medical check-up, you should:

  • Bring your identification documents, such as your NRIC or passport
  • Wear comfortable, loose-fitting clothing
  • Bring any necessary medical documents or medications
  • Get a good night's sleep before the exam
  • Avoid smoking or drinking alcohol before the exam

By understanding what to expect during the PDVL medical check-up and preparing appropriately, you can ensure that you meet the medical requirements for driving and obtain or renew your PDVL license smoothly.
