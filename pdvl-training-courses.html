PDVL Training Courses

If you're interested in becoming a private hire car driver in Singapore or want to improve your skills and knowledge as an existing driver, there are a variety of training courses available to help you. Here's an overview of the PDVL training courses:

PDVL Course Providers

There are several course providers in Singapore that offer PDVL training courses, including:

  • ComfortDelGro Driving Centre
  • NTUC LearningHub
  • Safe2Drive
  • TransCab Academy

These providers offer a range of courses that cover topics such as private hire car driving regulations, customer service, safety, and route planning.

PDVL Driver Skills

PDVL training courses can help drivers improve their skills in areas such as:

  • Route planning and navigation
  • Safe and defensive driving
  • Vehicle maintenance and inspection
  • Customer service and communication
  • Emergency response and crisis management

PDVL Customer Service

Good customer service is essential for private hire car drivers in Singapore. PDVL training courses can help drivers improve their customer service skills, such as:

  • Effective communication with passengers
  • Dealing with difficult passengers
  • Providing a safe and comfortable ride
  • Handling complaints and feedback

By investing in PDVL training courses, drivers can improve their skills, knowledge, and professionalism, which can lead to better job opportunities and higher earnings.
