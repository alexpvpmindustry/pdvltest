PDVL Test Preparation

If you're preparing for the Private Hire Car Driver's Vocational Licence (PDVL) test in Singapore, you'll want to make sure you're fully prepared. Here are some resources to help you study:

PDVL Study Materials

There are a variety of study materials available to help you prepare for the PDVL test. Here are a few options:

  • PDVL Handbook: The Land Transport Authority (LTA) provides a handbook that covers the test topics in detail. You can download a copy of the handbook from the LTA website.
  • Online resources: There are a number of websites and forums that offer study materials and practice tests for the PDVL test. Some popular options include Singapore Private Hire Car Drivers Association (SPDHA).
  • Training courses: Some training providers offer PDVL test preparation courses, which can provide additional support and guidance as you prepare for the test.

PDVL Practice Tests

Practice tests can be a valuable resource as you prepare for the PDVL test. They can help you get a feel for the test format and types of questions, as well as identify areas where you may need additional study. Here are a few options for practice tests:

  • PDVL.read-books.org: This website offers a variety of PDVL practice tests, including both Paper A and Paper B and Paper C.
  • SPDHA: The Singapore Private Hire Car Drivers Association also offers practice tests on their website, as well as a discussion forum where you can ask questions and connect with other drivers.
  • Training courses: Some training providers also offer PDVL practice tests as part of their courses.

PDVL Test Format and Passing Score

The PDVL test consists of 3 parts: Paper A and Paper B and Paper C. Paper A covers driving regulations, customer service, and safety, while Paper C covers route planning and map reading. The passing score for each paper is 17 out of 20.

Good luck with your PDVL test preparation!
