PDVL Regulations

If you're interested in becoming a private hire car driver in Singapore, it's important to understand the regulations and guidelines that govern this industry. Here's an overview of the PDVL regulations:

Licensing Requirements

To obtain a PDVL in Singapore, you must meet the following requirements:

  • Be at least 30 years old
  • Hold a valid Class 3/3A driving licence for at least 1 year
  • Pass the PDVL test
  • Complete the 10-hour PDVL training course
  • Undergo a medical check-up
  • Submit the PDVL application form and pay the application fee

Insurance Requirements

Private hire car drivers in Singapore are required to have commercial motor insurance that covers third-party liability, loss or damage to the vehicle, and personal accident coverage for the driver and passengers. The coverage must be at least $500,000 for third-party liability and $10,000 for personal accident coverage.

Safety Requirements

Private hire car drivers in Singapore are subject to a number of safety requirements, including:

  • Displaying a tamper-evident decal on the front and back windscreens of the vehicle
  • Maintaining a clean and well-maintained vehicle
  • Wearing proper attire and being well-groomed
  • Following traffic laws and regulations
  • Providing a child restraint device for passengers under 1.35m tall

By understanding and following these regulations, you can ensure that you are operating legally and safely as a private hire car driver in Singapore.
