PDVL Renewal Process

If you hold a Private Hire Car Driver's Vocational Licence (PDVL) in Singapore, you'll need to renew your license periodically to continue driving. Here's an overview of the PDVL renewal process:

Renewal Fee

The fee to renew your PDVL is currently S$40. You can pay the fee online or in person at the Land Transport Authority (LTA) office.

Documentation Requirements

When you renew your PDVL, you'll need to provide the following documentation:

  • A recent passport-sized photograph
  • A copy of your NRIC or passport
  • Your current PDVL
  • Proof of completion of the 10-hour PDVL refresher course (if your PDVL is expiring for the first time after 1 July 2020)

You can submit your documentation online or in person at the LTA office.

Renewal Deadlines

Your PDVL is valid for 6 years from the date of issue. You'll need to renew your PDVL before it expires to continue driving. The renewal process can be started up to 6 months before the expiry date.

If you fail to renew your PDVL before it expires, you'll need to retake the PDVL test and complete the 10-hour PDVL training course again to obtain a new PDVL.

By understanding the PDVL renewal process and taking action before your license expires, you can continue driving legally and avoid any disruptions to your business.
