PDVL Application Process

If you're interested in becoming a private hire car driver in Singapore, you'll need to apply for a Private Hire Car Driver's Vocational Licence (PDVL). Here are the steps involved in the application process:

  1. Meet the eligibility requirements: To be eligible for a PDVL, you must be at least 30 years old and have at least 1 year of driving experience in Singapore.
  2. Complete the training course: You'll need to complete a 10-hour training course on private hire car driving and regulations. You can take the course from any of the 3 approved training providers in Singapore.
  3. Submit the application form: You'll need to fill out and submit the PDVL application form, which is available online or in person at the Land Transport Authority (LTA) office.
  4. Provide the necessary documentation: You'll need to provide a recent passport-sized photograph, a copy of your NRIC or passport, and your driving licence. If you're a foreigner, you'll also need to provide proof of residency in Singapore.
  5. Pay the application fee: You'll need to pay a non-refundable application fee of S$40 when you submit your application.
  6. Attend the medical check-up: You'll need to undergo a medical check-up at a clinic approved by the LTA to ensure you're fit to drive.
  7. Wait for the outcome: The LTA will process your application and notify you of the outcome within 4-6 weeks.

If your application is approved, you'll need to attend a briefing session and collect your PDVL from the LTA office. You'll also need to obtain commercial motor insurance and install a tamper-evident decal on your vehicle.

Good luck with your application!
